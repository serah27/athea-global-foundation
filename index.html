Built on a decade of clinical expertise.

Athea Global was founded in 2021 as the non-profit arm of Hopewell Counseling—bringing professional mental health care to communities that cannot afford it.

We believe mental health care is not charity—it is justice. Today, we are a small, resilient team of therapists, coordinators, and advocates working across Kenya to break the silence around mental health.

The Hopewell Engine:

For every 10 therapy sessions purchased by corporate clients at Hopewell, Hopewell funds 1 free session through Athea for an underserved individual.
